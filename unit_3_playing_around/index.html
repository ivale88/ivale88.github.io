Visa

If you are going oversee for more than three months you probably need a student VISA. Different countries have very different rules about it, but the important thing is being aware of what is needed and knowing the process. How do you do that? Good news! Other have done it before you and processes don't change that much one year from the other. In this section you will be informed about all you need to know about VISAS and the system will help you to go through the whole process.

Healthcare

Don't stress out! These is usually strictly correlated to the VISA. Student VISAs or universities often provide healt insurance so you only need to go through a lot of bureaucracy but you will have it. In some countries this may not be the case.
