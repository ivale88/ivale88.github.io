Visa

If you go oversee for more than three months you probably need a student VISA. Different countries have very different rules, but the important thing is being aware of what is needed and knowing the process to get it (timing, documents, authorities, etc.). How can you do that? Good news for you: others have done it before you and processes remain similar over time. In this section you will find all information you need to obtain your VISA and we will help you to go through the whole process.

Housing

Finding a house or a room may sound like an easy one, but there are many hidden obstacles you will encounter or you have already encountered. In many places it may be hard to rent convenient places for less than one year, and what if you want to keep your place for when you are coming back?

Healthcare

Don't stress out! These is usually strictly correlated to the VISA. Student VISAs or universities often provide healt insurance so you only need to go through a lot of bureaucracy but you will have it. In some countries this may not be the case. Here you will find all information you need to safely get sick abroad!

City Survival Kit

What is the local currency? What pluggers do they have there? Are tips mandatory? Can I drink alcohol in the street? What are the low cost airlines there? How many questions are you asking yourself about the new place? Test youself with our quizzes and learn all you need to know about your new temporary home.
